Potato Omelette

The unique and incomparable potato omelette... The never-ending fight of without onion or with onion. 9/10.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Onion
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. Peel the potatoes and cut them into thin slices, making sure that all of them are of uniform size. Leave them in water for 15 minutes and put a frying pan with plenty of olive oil on the fire.
  2. Remove the potatoes and drain them well from the oil and put them in a large bowl. Drain the onion when it is ready, and put it on top of the potatoes.
  3. Beat the eggs and add them to the bowl, stirring with a fork to mix the three ingredients well.
